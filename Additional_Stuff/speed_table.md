# Mechanics

- **HP**: starts at 100 per run. Risk failure at speed ≤8 deals damage instead of instant crash.
- **Break**: when HP ≤ 0, car is broken. A repair mini-game appears — click the hammer 10 times to fix it.
- **Instant crash**: speed 9-10 (90-100 km/h) has a 50% risk of instantly blowing the engine (no damage step).
- **Nitro**: +2 extra steps per Go, costs 15 extra fuel, 20% chance of 30 HP damage.
- **Fuel**: consumption = speed × 2. Tank starts at 100.

# Upgrades (max 3 levels each)

| Upgrade | Effect          | Cost |
|---------|-----------------|------|
| Engine  | -10% crash risk | 3 scraps |
| Tank    | +25 fuel cap    | 2 scraps |
| Armor   | -50% damage     | 4 scraps |
| Turbo   | +1 step/Go      | 5 scraps |

- **Scraps**: earn 1 per finished run.
- **Streak**: consecutive steps in one run. Best streak saved per session.
